About the Project

AstroLens is built to help beginners explore the skies through the lens of a camera. We simplify the complex techniques of astrophotography into bite-sized, beginner-friendly tutorials.

Goals

  • Demystify night sky photography
  • Offer visual guides for practical learning
  • Support aspiring photographers through the basics

History Highlights

From the first moon photo in 1840 to the Hubble Telescope, the journey of astrophotography has revolutionized our view of the universe. Explore this incredible timeline through our curated lessons.
